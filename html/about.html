About Me


I’ve deployed twice in my 6-year career in the United States Army to Kuwait where I worked at the Port of Shuaiba and also in Iraq in accordance with Operation Inherent Resolve and Operation Resolute Support in regard to Logistics and Transportation Operations. On my second deployment, I had the opportunity to be part of Defender 2020 and Operation Atlantic Resolve where I deployed to Germany and worked at the Port of Gdynia in Poland and the Netherlands at the Port of Rotterdam. I was also given the opportunity to oversee and plan operations of 77 military helicopters estimating 30 million USD each and in addition to 1,500 pieces of other military vehicles. In both my mobilizations to the Middle East and Europe I’ve moved over 8 billion USD of military cargo, including hazmat materials. Along with my two mobilizations I’ve had missions in Hawaii at Port Harbor and many states in the United States. I’ve managed and supervised teams in these military operations and currently hold the title of Non-Commissioned officer in charge (NCOIC) of ICODES (Integrated Computerized Deployment System) and Security Officer in the 1174th DDSB under the SDDC (Surface Distribution Deployment Command) where I currently reside in the United States Army Reserve.

I would describe myself to be a disciplined, motivated, educated, and hardworking individual who will always keep up with high expectations. I seek to meet the standards and raise those bars. I take pride in my pursuit to prove myself in the work environment and show that I am capable of higher positions. On a personal level, I will always qualify myself for a situation that is to the utmost challenging and will test my abilities. I have acquired the ability to be a fast learner in any environment and work under pressure with the added abilities to adapt and overcome ordeals to find proactive ways for ramifications.
